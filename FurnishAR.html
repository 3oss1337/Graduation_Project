Pipeline

How it works

From a single photo to a production-ready 3D model in under 5 seconds.

Upload

Any single photo of furniture — no special lighting or background needed.

Background removal

rembg automatically isolates the furniture using a neural segmentation model (U²-Net).

TripoSR inference

Transformer-based model generates a full 3D occupancy field from the single image view.

Mesh extraction

Marching Cubes algorithm converts the occupancy field into a polygon mesh with UV textures.

Interactive viewer

Three.js renders the mesh in a real-time WebGL viewer — rotate, zoom, inspect wireframe.

Export

Download as OBJ or GLB — compatible with SketchUp, Revit, Blender, AutoCAD, and AR tools.

What is FurnishAR?

FurnishAR is a graduation project that uses TripoSR — a transformer-based AI model — to reconstruct fully interactive 3D meshes from a single 2D furniture image. Designed specifically for interior designers, it lets you visualize any piece of furniture in 3D and place it inside real room photos before making purchasing or design decisions.
